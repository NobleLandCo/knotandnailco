About Us

Built to Last

Knot & Nail Co. started in 2026 in Puyallup, WA with a simple idea: build things that last. Every piece is made by hand in our shop using quality materials, designed for real life in the Pacific Northwest.

No assembly lines. No shortcuts. Just wood, tools, and the kind of work you can pass down.

"Build things that last. That's it. That's the whole idea."
