## Business Description (750 chars max)
Knot & Nail Co. is a small father-and-son woodworking shop handcrafting quality wood pieces right here in Puyallup, WA. We specialize in personalized kids growth charts, mud kitchens, pet furniture, dog dish stands, and custom builds for families and pet owners throughout the South Sound.

Every piece is made from real wood — no particle board, no shortcuts. We love what we build and it shows. Whether you're looking for a milestone keepsake, a backyard play feature, or something completely custom, we'd love to make it for you.

DM us or visit our website to browse products and request a quote. Local pickup available. Custom orders welcome.
